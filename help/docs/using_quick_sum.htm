Using Quick Sum

         

Performing Standard Data Operations > Grouping and Summarizing Data > Using Quick Sum

 

In addition to summarizing an entire table, Tabular IQ also offers a quick, ad hoc tool for finding sums within your table.  You can automatically calculate a sum by highlighting a selection of numbers within the datasheet.

 

 

As you highlight cells within a column or multiple columns, the sum of those numbers will automatically appear in the Status bar.  For example, in the picture above, the sum of the highlighted numbers is 158.6200.
