Importing and Exporting Data

         

Accessing and Saving Data > Importing and Exporting Data

 

In addition to opening and saving tables and connecting directly to database systems, Tabular IQ also offers the ability to import and export data sets.

 

The Import and Export functions provide a more formal way of getting data in and out of the software.  Unlike open and save, it lets you perform the import/export routine on multiple files at once.  In addition, it enables you to push data in and out of database systems, like MySQL, Oracle and SQL Server, without first creating a connection from your Project.
