Merging and Dividing Tables

         

Performing Standard Data Operations > Merging and Dividing Tables

 

Sometimes, before you can get to your analysis, you may need to perform certain data integration operations.  On the front end, this may mean taking multiple data sets and merging them together.  On the back end, this may mean taking a large data set in Tabular IQ and splitting it out into multiple sets to use with other software applications with data size limitations.

 

This section will discuss the utilities that Tabular IQ offers for these two operations.
